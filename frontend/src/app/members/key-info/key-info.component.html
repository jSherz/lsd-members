The really important bits

This stuff is really important for your static line course weekend. Please read it all!

Pack light
Make sure you bring plenty of warm clothes but please don't bring a huge hard suitcase. Your driver is more likely to turn up in a Micra than a 4x4.
Don't forget cash
Unlike in most of the developed world, you'll struggle to pay for anything other than the jump tickets with a credit card so please bring at least £30-40 with you for the weekend.
No really, bring warm clothes
We've said it once, and we'll say it again (and again... and again...) please bring lots of warm clothes and a warm sleeping bag. Even if the weather looks warm for a weekend, you will need warm clothes. Every year a new student tells us that it's colder in the bunkhouse (your accommodation) than outside. They're not wrong.
Don't forget sturdy shoes
Trainers are the ideal but you must not bring walking boots or shoes with hooks. They might get a little muddy (sorry) but it's worth it for the skydiving!
Veggie? Vegan? Dietary requirements?
Langar isn't fantastic for vegetarians, vegans or effectively anyone that doesn't like a greasy fry up with bacon & eggs etc. If you have any dietary requirements, we recommend you bring some food that will help top up whatever you eat on site.
